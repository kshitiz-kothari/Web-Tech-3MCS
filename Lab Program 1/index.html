Chess

Chess is a recreational and competitive board game played between two players. It is sometimes called Western or international chess to distinguish it from related games such as xiangqi.

Advantages of playing chess

Chess increases your intelligence

- People with lots of experience playing chess have highly developed thinking abilities in two areas (in addition to memory skills):

  • Fluid intelligence

    This is the ability to consider new kinds of problems and use reasoning to solve them.

  • Processing speed

    This is the ability to swiftly comprehend tasks and respond efficiently to challenges.
